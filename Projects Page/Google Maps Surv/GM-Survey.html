During Weeks 3, 4 and 5 we had to make a survey concerning people's perception on a specific application. We have chosen to have a look over Google Maps. Our approach was as follows:

  • Check the age of our users
  • Check which means of transport they use
  • Create Personas
  • See when/how they use Google Maps
  • See how satisfied are they

Personas

In oreder to gain more information and to have a better look over what Google Maps has to offer we have decided to create personas.
