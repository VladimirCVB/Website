Website Prototype - Hotel Express

For this assignment we had to create a travelling/booking website. Our target users were families of 4 people, usually with young children.

After receiving the feedback from our users we have found that:

Likes
  • The information is very easy to follow, being shown in a minimalistic manner.
  • It has error prevention allowing the user to correct mistakes, typos or wrong dates
  • It gives the user freedom, browsing throughout the site with no constraints
  • The information is easy to discover due to the fact that it has easy and understandable controls
Dislikes
  • The reviews in this domain are important and therefore they should be more visible
  • The “Book Now” button it is in the description area which I think it is not normal and natural
  • Also an “Average stars” (accorded by users) area should be included in the hotel page and in the selection page (the page where the user selects the hotel
